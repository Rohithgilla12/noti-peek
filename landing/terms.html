§ 2What noti-peek is

A native macOS menubar app that fetches notifications from providers you connect (GitHub, Linear, Jira, Bitbucket) via their official APIs and OAuth flows, bundles them, and displays them locally. A small Cloudflare Worker brokers the OAuth dance and forwards API calls using your tokens. No messages, PRs, issues, or comment bodies are stored on the server — see the privacy statement for exact data handling.

§ 3Your account

There is no user account in the traditional sense. The app generates an anonymous device_token on first launch and uses that to identify your install to the backend. You are responsible for keeping your device and its stored tokens secure. If your device is compromised, disconnect providers in-app (which revokes tokens with the provider) or email security@notipeek.dev with your device token for immediate server-side purge.
